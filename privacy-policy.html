Privacy Policy

Last Moment Tutions respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our services.

We collect the following types of personal information:

3. Use of Information

Your information is used solely for the purpose of fulfilling your paper request, processing payments, and communicating updates regarding your orders.

4. Data Security

We take appropriate security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction.

5. Sharing of Information

We do not share your personal information with third parties except for payment processing via Razorpay. We may also share information if required by law.

6. Cookies

We may use cookies to enhance your user experience. You can choose to disable cookies in your browser settings, though this may affect website functionality.

7. Your Rights

You can request to access, update, or delete your personal information by contacting us directly. We will respond to your request within a reasonable time frame.

8. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and the effective date will be updated.
